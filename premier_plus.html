Premier plus membership is a product anchored at high-frequency, medium/high-penetration (penetration is an indicator of a user’s neediness of premier ride; penetration = premier orders / total orders with DiDi) users. As membership period is on monthly basis, we use their first-purchasing time as node to lock their demand for premier ride in the future months. The below six benefits are included:

  • 1. Discounts: coupon package, for one-time discount;
  • 2. Dynamic price overflow free (price overflow exists in setting where demand is far over supply): price overflow exempt on supply-demand imbalance scenarios;
  • 3. Super speed order-taken: order been taken with priorities to save waiting time;
  • 4. Driver red packet: build up ecosystem of thankfulness and trust that users can thank driver for their service with the red packet;
  • 5. Airport pick-up service: for business people we provide guide at the arrival level;
  • 6. Exclusive customer service: membership-only customer service team;
